Who We Are

About Us

The Coffee Hut, located at Mindspace, Haggerston, and The Huckletree, was founded by three passionate coffee enthusiasts. Over time, it has grown into a dedicated team of skilled professionals who bring extensive expertise and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Since opening on Leather Lane, The Coffee Hut has remained a beloved destination for locals and an inviting stop for visitors from around the world. Guided by curiosity, we continuously explore new ingredients and techniques, while staying true to our core belief: serving coffee and food that bring joy, complemented by service that leaves every guest with a smile.

What We Do

What We Do

At The Coffee Hut, we are passionate about supporting local producers and small-scale growers.

We proudly serve specialty coffee from trusted roasters, along with carefully selected guest roasters from across Europe and beyond. Our menu is always evolving — from fruity natural Ethiopians to sweet washed Colombians, there’s something to delight every coffee lover. We also share a love for tea, sourcing from small, independent farms to bring you unique and delicate flavors.

In addition to our coffee and tea, The Coffee Hut Kitchen offers a delicious range of seasonal, home-cooked food for breakfast and lunch. We work with reputable local suppliers to ensure that every dish is fresh, flavorful, and thoughtfully prepared — the perfect pairing with your favorite brew.
