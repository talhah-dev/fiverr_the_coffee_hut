Who We Are

About Us

Founded in 2020, The Coffee Hutis an independent cafe created to bring convenience and quality together for professionals rightinside your workplace. We serve freshly brewed Origin coffee, freshly prepared breakfasts and delicious lunches. In addition to our daily menu, we offer highly personalized catering for meetings, corporate events, and private gatherings, with menus tailored to suit every occasion.

Fresh & Flavorful

Food & Drinks

At The Coffee Hut, we serve a wide range of delicious options — from open toasts and bagels to freshly made focaccias and vibrant poke bowls. Our menu features vegan and vegetarian choices, all made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick coffee, breakfast, or a relaxed lunch, we’ve got something for everyone.

Our Services

Catering

We offer a wide range of catering services tailored to your needs, from breakfast and lunch to after-work refreshments. Our chef specializes in a variety of cuisines and will create dishes perfectly suited to your preferences and taste.
